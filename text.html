MGMT 335

Managing Individuals, Teams, and Organizations

Theories and concepts related to human behavior in organizations at the Individuals, interpersonal, group, and organizational levels. Application of these theories and concepts to managerial problems and opportunites.

Prerequisites & Notes

PRQ: MGMT 333 or UBUS 310 and UBUS 311, or consent of department.

FINA 320

Principles of Finance

Principles underlying managerial decisions influenced by capital structure and type of ownership; problems related to sources of financing; the tools of financial analysis and financial management; integration of economic theory and accounting.

Prerequisites & Notes

PRQ: ACCY 206 or ACCY 288, and UBUS 223 or STAT 208 or STAT 301 or STAT 350; and sophomore standing.
